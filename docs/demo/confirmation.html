Module 2 · Confirmation & progress report sheet

You’re registered ✓

Each attendee gets one A4 progress report sheet: the Care Spine rail shows their stations in routing order; the header QR is the staff scan target; the footer QR is the patient’s take-home link. The patient keeps the sheet — dcica stores only identity and paid services, purged after camp.

What happens next
  • ✓ A confirmation email goes to the registrant with each attendee’s QR check-in code.
  • ✓ On camp day, present the QR (or look up by name) at Check-In.
  • ✓ Pre-registered sheets are printed in color the night before; walk-ins print live at the desk.
