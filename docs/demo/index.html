MedCamp — MVP walk-through

A clickable mockup of three flows — Patient, Volunteer, and Vendor. It’s static: nothing is sent anywhere, so the committee can fill forms, watch totals, and route people through, then tell us what to change before we build.

Sample data

Load a completed record into all three modules so the confirmation and lookup screens show realistic content right away — or start empty and fill the forms yourself (each form also has a “Fill with sample data” button).

Patient flow

Online registration through to the coordinator dashboard.

Module 1
Registration & payment
Registrant + any number of attendees. Live pricing, capacity caps, minor waivers, consent.
Module 2
Progress report sheet
One A4 sheet per attendee — Care Spine routing rail, QR header, paid services, camp ID.
Module 3
Check-in gate
Look up by code or name → Registered / Paid / Waiver. Minors without a guardian waiver are blocked.
Module 4
Station queue
Phone-first. Tap a patient Queued → In progress → Done. Add-ons flag “needs payment.”
Module 5
Coordinator dashboard
Counts, queue depths, needs-payment, and the walk-in registration open/hold toggle.
Bonus
Public “Who you saw today”
The take-home QR destination: thanks the medical staff and invites a review.

Volunteer flow

Recruitment → signup → confirmation → day-of sign in/out → certificate. Profiles persist across events (no PHI).

Sign up
Volunteer signup
Role preferences with capacity caps and age-group gating; minor consent; skills & emergency contact.
Confirm
Confirmation + sign-in QR
Assigned role, shift, arrival time, role brief, and the day-of sign-in QR.
Coordinator
Roster & sign in/out
Capacity vs. target, signups by source, day-of sign in/out with automatic hours.
Thank you
Certificate
Auto-generated certificate — name, role, hours — printable / save as PDF.

Vendor flow

Apply → committee review → Zelle payment → booth assignment. Vendors pay by Zelle/check (no card fees).

Apply
Vendor application
Business + contact, vendor type and space, with the fee calculated live.
Review
Committee review
Approve (sends Zelle instructions) → mark payment received → assign a booth from the grid.
Confirm
Vendor confirmation
Booth number, setup window, and fee-paid summary the vendor receives.

Logistics & operations

The committee/coordinator tools around the camp — procurement, run sheet, lab mailing, and venue layout.

Module 6
Supply calculator
Registered counts per service → procurement list by category, with overrides. Printable.
Module 7
Operations checklist
Phase-based run sheet with roles, plus the printable signs the system generates.
Module 8
Lab tracking & mailing
Mark reports received → print address labels → mark mailed. No clinical data stored.
Venue
Venue configuration
Switch between the Clinic and Open-space layouts — same software, different floor plan.

What this demo is not

  • · No real Stripe/Zelle payment, email, or database — every action is local to your browser.
  • · No PHI: no diagnoses, results, or clinical notes — by design, same as the real system.
  • · Visuals follow the project Design System (Care Spine, saffron = action, the type trio).

Open questions for the committee live at the end of docs/MedCamp-System-Overview.md — this demo makes those concrete.
