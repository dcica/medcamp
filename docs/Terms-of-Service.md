## 1. About dcica

dcica is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that organizes medical camps and community events. Registration and payments are processed through this platform on behalf of dcica.

---

## 2. Event Registration

**Payment:** Registration is confirmed only after payment is received in full. We do not hold spots without payment.

**Cancellations & Refunds:**
- Cancellation before camp day: full refund via original payment method
- No-shows: no automatic refund; contact sachin@buzzclan.com within 7 days
- Service unavailable on the day: refund for that service only
- Family registrations: all members can be cancelled together, or individual members cancelled separately

**Walk-ins:** Walk-in registration is available subject to capacity. Pre-registration is strongly recommended.

**Services:** Services listed at registration are subject to availability on camp day. In the rare event a service cannot be provided, a refund for that service will be issued.

---

## 3. Medical Camp Waiver

All participants in medical camp events must sign a waiver before receiving services. The waiver:
- Acknowledges that services are provided by volunteer medical professionals
- Confirms that no diagnosis, prescription, or treatment record is retained in our system
- Grants permission for volunteer staff to assist with non-clinical logistics (routing, check-in, etc.)

A separate waiver document is presented at registration. Digital signature on a tablet or handwritten signature on paper are both accepted.

---

## 4. No Medical Records

**dcica does not store medical records, diagnoses, lab results, or clinical notes.** This platform is a logistics and payment system only. Any clinical documentation is the responsibility of the attending medical professional and is not retained by dcica.

Lab results are returned by the testing lab as physical paper documents and mailed to you. We track only the mailing status (received / mailed) — not the content of the results.

**Cancellation:** Membership fees are non-refundable after purchase.
